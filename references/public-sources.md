## Search Findings

No separate public administrator manual PDF or help-center style manual was found through web search. The official site describes features rather than step-by-step back-office operation.

Relevant public feature areas from the official site:

- class schedule and reservation management
- real-time reservation, waitlist, cancellation, and closure status
- class registration and class-member lookup
- private and group ticket/product management
- member search and member detail with ticket/payment/use history
- facility business hours, instructor work hours, holidays, and special-day settings
- reservation/cancellation/operation policy settings
- role-based system permissions
- sales, points, and reports

## Practical Implication

For automation, the reusable manual must be built from:

1. public feature descriptions,
2. read-only inventory of the logged-in admin site,
3. dry-run scripts and screenshots kept only in local debug folders,
4. confirmed live-operation results written back to the work sheet.
